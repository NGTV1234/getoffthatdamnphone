Get off that damn phone

In a world of technology and innovation, we delve so deep into meaningless apps that are meant to stimulate our brains (doomscrolling), that we forget why we open the app in the first place. While it may not look like it these days, there's still a whole world out there.

Ever wonder why life seemed much brighter, colorful, & more joyful when you were a kid? It's because it was. You didn't spend your childhood looking at LED panels light up, you spent it looking at true color. Your parents are right, it is that damn phone.

After pressing start, you will see the whole screen turn to the color "Pantone 448 C", deemed the ugliest color. The screen will remain that way for a duration between 4 - 7 minutes. Your objective is to only look at the screen, until the screen changes.

Once successful, go outside, and see the true colors of life. Ready?

You did it. Was that so hard?
